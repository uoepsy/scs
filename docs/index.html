Short Courses in Statistics

How it works/What we offer

To support PhD students in our department, we are holding a series of short workshops (1-3 days) which take place every year. These will be a mix of lectures and practical sessions. You can find a list of these workshops below.
In addition, we have a Piazza forum which we monitor year-round, as a place for PhD students to ask any statistics-related questions, share bits of code with which they are struggling, etc.
